About Me

I started coding so I could have the means and the freedom to do the things I truly love. I quickly discovered that coding was one of those things! Building websites feeds my need for creativity as well as my need for instant gratification. I love to see my progress in real time! I also love the fact that it lets me showcase my strong attention to detail, organization skills and creative problem solving.


I fancy myself as a mimic artist. I can look at a project and figure out how to re-create it by disecting it into steps that make sense to me and what I already know how to do.
